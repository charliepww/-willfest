Where to sleep

There are 4 different camps that you can stay in, blue camp, green camp, brown camp and red camp. Each camp has three sets of 12 toilets and 5 food trucks each.

Where to eat

Each camp will have one vegan food truck, one pizza truck, one burger truck, a hot drinks truck (free hot drinks if you have bought willfest merchandise or have vip ticket) and a kebab truck. There is also the food row just before the arenas and around the edge of the main stage.

Safety

To prevent harm to anyone we would like to ask you to not bring any glass bottles, if you are seen with glass bottles upon entrance to the event they will be taken off you and disposed of. This is the same as if you are inside with the bottle.

Recycling

Any paper cups and cans that get handed into the recycling truck to the left of the side entrance to the alternative stage, each cup and can you hand in we will give you 5p.
